# Justice Matters - Addiction Support

A supportive online community for families who have lost loved ones to addiction. This platform provides a safe space for families to share their grief, connect with others who understand their pain, and seek justice together.

## About

Justice Matters is dedicated to families who have suffered the devastating loss of a loved one due to addiction, especially in cases where:
- Individuals were given something different than what they asked for
- Loved ones were intentionally harmed through tainted substances
- Someone put something in their drink or food to take their lives

No one's life should be taken, no matter what. Together, we can support each other and seek justice for our loved ones.

## Getting Started

### Local Development

1. Clone this repository:
```bash
git clone https://github.com/Nevertheless27/JusticeMatters-Addiction.git
cd JusticeMatters-Addiction
```

2. Open `index.html` in your web browser:
```bash
# On macOS
open index.html

# On Linux
xdg-open index.html

# On Windows
start index.html
```

Or simply double-click the `index.html` file in your file explorer.

### Using the Platform

1. **Visit the Home Page**: Start at `index.html` to understand our mission
2. **Join the Support Chat**: Click "Support Chat" to connect with others in real-time
3. **Share Your Story**: Visit "Family Stories" to share your loved one's memory
4. **Access Resources**: Find helpful information and contacts on the "Resources" page

## Technology

This is a static website built with:
- **HTML5** - Structure and content
- **CSS3** - Styling and responsive design
- **Vanilla JavaScript** - Interactive features
- **Local Storage** - Client-side data persistence

No server or database is required. All data is stored locally in the user's browser.

## Privacy & Safety

- All chat messages are stored locally on your device
- Stories are stored locally and only visible to those using your browser

# JusticeMatters-Addiction

This project is dedicated to seeking justice and support for families who have lost loved ones to addiction and related mental health struggles. We believe that no life should be lost to these preventable tragedies, and we are committed to raising awareness, providing resources, and advocating for systemic change.

Our mission is rooted in compassion and understanding. We recognize that addiction is a disease, not a moral failing, and that those affected‚Äîboth the individuals struggling and their families‚Äîdeserve dignity, respect, and access to proper care and legal recourse when negligence or misconduct occurs.

## Why This Matters

- **Awareness**: Many families suffer in silence, unaware of their rights or the resources available to them.
- **Resources**: Access to support groups, counseling, and legal aid can make a crucial difference in the healing process.
- **Advocacy**: We work to highlight systemic issues in healthcare, criminal justice, and social services that contribute to preventable deaths.
- **Legal Support**: Families deserve justice when loved ones receive improper care, are given substances different from what was prescribed, or are victims of negligence.
- **Prevention**: By sharing stories and data, we aim to prevent future tragedies and improve care standards.
